How It Works

  1. Create an exam here and click Save & Generate Exam File → downloads as a .json file.
  2. Share the .json file with your students (USB, email, Google Classroom, etc.).
  3. Students go to student-exam.html on your website, upload the file, and complete the exam.
  4. When finished, students click Download My Result → saves a result .json file.
  5. Students submit their result file to you. You upload it here in the Import Results tab.
  6. View all results, track scores, violations, and download a CSV report.
