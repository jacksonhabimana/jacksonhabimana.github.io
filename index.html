About This Portal

This chemistry education portal was built to make high-quality chemistry learning accessible to every Rwandan secondary school student. Each course follows the official REB syllabus exactly, presented in a modern, engaging format.

Every unit includes full notes with embedded diagrams, multiple worked examples, an interactive 25-question quiz with instant scoring, and a 50-mark structured test with model answers.

Whether you’re revising for national exams or learning a concept for the first time, this portal has everything you need — completely free.
