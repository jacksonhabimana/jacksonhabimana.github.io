📚 Exam Tips from Mr Habimana

Read every question carefullyLook for command words: state, explain, describe, calculate, suggest. Each has a specific expected response style.
Show all workingIn calculation questions, even a wrong final answer earns marks if your method is correct. Never skip steps.
Use correct unitskJ mol⁻¹, mol dm⁻³, nm — always include units with numerical answers. Missing units loses marks.
Learn equations by heartKey equations (Haber, Ostwald, Contact processes, neutralisation, combustion) must be balanced correctly.
Manage your timeAllocate roughly 1 mark per minute. Skip difficult questions and return. Don't get stuck on one part.
Practice past papersThe most effective revision is doing actual exam questions under timed conditions and reviewing model answers.
